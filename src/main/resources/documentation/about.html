About Inspector

 

My name is Kelly Ryan and Inspector is my Final Year Project for my BSc. In Computer Systems from University of Limerick.

Project work commenced in September 2021 and was completed in April 2022 and was supervised by Dr Patrick Healy of the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems at University of Limerick.

It is written in Java SE 11 and uses JavaFX 18, SQLite 3, RichTextFX 0.10.9 and Apache Commons CSV 1.9.0.    

Full code is available at: https://github.com/ULKellyRyan/Inspector-2022
